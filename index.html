Minecraft is a popular sandbox video game that allows players to explore, create, and survive in a virtual world made up of blocks. It was created by Swedish developer Markus Persson, also known as "Notch," and later developed and published by Mojang Studios.

The game has been released on multiple platforms, including PC, consoles, mobile devices, and virtual reality, allowing players to enjoy Minecraft across various devices and play with friends through online multiplayer or local networks.
